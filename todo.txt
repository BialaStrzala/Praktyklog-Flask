Python+Flask app that keeps student internship information in a database
- Roles: Students, supervisors (from the college and the workplace), admins 
- Students fill out their information (eg. internship start date) and keep and update an "internship log" table (day of internship, date, description, and a signing from the student's assigned supervisor) 
- Supervisors can see their assigned students' logs and sign their daily logs - Admins can view all users, etc 
- Users need to log in, then they can access their dashboards

ROUTES:
- / -> logowanie
- /student/dashboard
- /student/profil
- /student/dziennik -> zobacz dziennik, edytuj/dodaj nowy wpis
- /opiekun/dashboard
